## Design System & Styling

### Consistent Standards Applied to ALL Pages

#### Typography
- **Primary Text**: `text-black` (never white or light colors)
- **Secondary Text**: `text-gray-600`
- **Headers**: `text-2xl font-bold text-black` (page titles)
- **Table Headers**: `text-xs font-bold text-black uppercase`
- **Breadcrumbs**: `text-sm text-gray-600`

#### Colors & Styling
- **Containers**: `glass-card` (backdrop-blur-md bg-white/40)
- **Borders**: `border border-gray-300` (professional gray)
- **Hover Effects**: `hover:bg-gray-100` or `hover:bg-white/20`
- **Badges**: Color-coded by status (green=success, red=danger, yellow=warning, blue=info)
- **Buttons**: Blue primary (`bg-blue-500`), gray secondary, red danger

#### Layout
- **Container Padding**: `p-6` (standard card padding)
- **Vertical Spacing**: `space-y-6` (section gaps)
- **Responsive Grid**: `grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3` (3-column on desktop)
- **Table Styling**: `border-b border-gray-300` (headers), `border-b border-gray-200` (rows)
- **Gaps**: `gap-6` (grid/flex gaps)
